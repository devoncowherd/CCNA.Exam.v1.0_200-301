Interfaces (Cables and Connectors)

What are Interfaces and Cables?

Interfaces and Cables are the physical cables that connect network hardware components. While many hardware components are wiresless today (Phones, Laptops, and more), there is still a massive grid of cabling typing many businesses and personal computers to the net


There are several types of popular cable types and jacks that are in use today.
